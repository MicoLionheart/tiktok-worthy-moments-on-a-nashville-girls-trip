TikTok-Worthy Moments on a Nashville Girls' Trip

Make your Nashville girls' trip unforgettable with these TikTok-worthy moments curated by Nashville Experience Tours.

1. Dancing on Broadway

Neon Lights and Live Music

Broadway’s vibrant energy is the perfect backdrop for a TikTok dance challenge. Capture your group dancing in front of the neon lights or outside iconic honky-tonks for a video that screams “Music City.”

2. The “I Believe in Nashville” Mural

Iconic and Inspirational

No Nashville trip is complete without a stop at the famous “I Believe in Nashville” mural. Gather your girls for a fun and stylish group shot that’s sure to rack up the likes.

3. Rooftop Views at FGL House

Skyline and Selfies

Head to the rooftop of FGL House for stunning views of downtown Nashville. Whether it’s a golden hour selfie or a group cheers with cocktails, this spot is TikTok gold.

4. Line Dancing at Wildhorse Saloon

Show Off Your Moves

Film your group learning line dancing at Wildhorse Saloon. It’s a fun and interactive way to capture the spirit of Nashville while creating a video that’s perfect for TikTok.

5. The Gulch’s Angel Wings Mural

Strike a Pose

The angel wings mural in The Gulch is one of Nashville’s most Instagrammable spots, but it’s also perfect for TikTok. Create a slow-motion video of your group walking toward the mural for a dramatic and trendy effect.

6. Party Bus Adventures

Music, Lights, and Fun

Book a party bus with Nashville Experience Tours and capture the fun as you cruise through the city. With music, lights, and your best friends, it’s a guaranteed viral moment.

7. Sunset on the Pedestrian Bridge

Golden Hour Perfection

The John Seigenthaler Pedestrian Bridge offers breathtaking views of the Nashville skyline at sunset. Film a slow pan of the cityscape or a group shot with the glowing sky as your backdrop.

8. Honky-Tonk Bar Crawl

Cheers to the Night

Document your honky-tonk bar crawl as you explore Nashville’s nightlife. Capture snippets of live music, dancing, and toasts with your girls for a fun and dynamic TikTok montage.
